About Our Bishop
Rt. Rev. Dr. Felix Orji

At Holy Trinity Anglican Church, Nashville, part of the Anglican Diocese of All Nations., we are blessed to be led by our dedicated pastor, Rev. Canon Samuel Igwe, A faithful servant of God, He brings a wealth of experience, spiritual wisdom, and a deep passion for teaching God’s Word. Known for their engaging sermons and heart for discipleship, He inspires us to grow in faith and live Christ-centered lives. Their leadership emphasizes prayer, humility, and the transformative power of the gospel, making Scripture relatable and impactful for daily living.
