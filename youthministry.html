Youth & Young Adults

The Youth and Young Adult Ministry provides a space for young people to be challenged in their faith and committed to living for Christ while excelling academically. The ministry encourages active involvement in church life and fosters connections with other Christian youth. Young Adults are empowered to pursue their personal and professional goals, while also learning valuable insights on building healthy relationships and preparing for marriage. Additionally, they are engaged in social outreach and community service, embodying Christ’s love in action.
