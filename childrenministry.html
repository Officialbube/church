Children’s Ministries

The Children’s Ministry at Holy Trinity Anglican Church is a place where children are taught the word of God in a fun and interactive way. We believe that children are a gift from God and we are committed to helping them grow in the knowledge of God’s word. Our Children’s Ministry is designed to help children develop a personal relationship with Jesus Christ and to help them grow in their faith. We offer a variety of programs and activities for children of all ages, including Sunday School, Children’s Church, Vacation Bible School, and more. Our goal is to provide a safe and nurturing environment where children can learn about God’s love and grace and grow in their faith.
